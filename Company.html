Our Company

Shri Knitwear is a reputable and fast growing knitwear company based in Tirupur, India. Shri Knitwear was established in the year 2010 with a dedicated team of highly experienced and professional people from the knitwear manufacturing industry. We engage in manufacturing and Exporting knitted garments for various retail brands and chain stores across the globe. We offer our clients with products of impeccable quality, on-time delivery, competitive price, reliable and committed service & more

"Our aim is to supply quality fashion products at a competitive price to brands across the globe”
