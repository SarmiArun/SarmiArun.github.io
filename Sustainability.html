Sustainability

Shri Knitwear has implemented various technologies in our factories for sustainable garment production, wherein 98% of our products are manufactured using sustainable cotton.

We have incorporated solar panels for green energy in our factory premises which adds further to our approach towards sustainability, additionally we have implemented methods to conserve water through rain water harvesting.

We regularly audit our supply chain process to maintain our high environmental standards in our production, and we have timely external audits carried out to update our factory certifications which are GOTS, OEKO TEX, BCI. Our Ethical working standards are regularly monitored by our SEDEX audit and certifications.

Our clients are providing valuable support with various new ideas, new product specifications and techniques which helps us to move towards sustainable fashion.
